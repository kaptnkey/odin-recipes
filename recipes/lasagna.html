Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a filling layered pasta. It includes the pasta base. A meat usually beef or pork, mixed with herbs and spices. topped with cheese and tomato sauce. Then another layer of pasta added and topped with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
