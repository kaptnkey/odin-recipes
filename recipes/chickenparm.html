Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken parmesan or chicken parm as it is called more often is breaded chicken covered in cheese, garnish, and of course, parmesan. The chicken is then layed over pasta usually spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic cloves
  • Tomatoes canned
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Parsley
  • Chicken cutlets
  • Vegetable oil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • Basil
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. set a wire rack on baking sheet
  4. Put chicken on to clean baking sheet and put mozzarella on chicken and bake
  5. Make the pasta
  6. top the chicken with parmesan and basil serve on top of spaghetti
