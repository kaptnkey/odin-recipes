Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A simple dish and very well known. Some pasta, some seasoning, some milk, some cheese and a boiling pot and you can make a good filling dish

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil lightly salted water in pot
  2. Melt butter in saucepan
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper to saucepan
  4. Pour in milk slowly while cooking and stirring
  5. Add cheddar cheese and stir until melted
  6. Drain macaroni and pour into cheese sauce
  7. Serve hot!
